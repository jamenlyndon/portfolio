BuRPS

Design and development of a progressive web app for a large Australian SaaS company.

BuRPS (Budgeting, Rostering and Payroll Software) is used to run over 250 IGA Supermarkets and many other businesses across Australia.

The app, called the "BuRPS Staff Portal" is used for all communication between thousands of employees and their employers. It allows staff to view and approve their rosters, request time off, complete required training and much more.

Both the design and functionality are inline with the Google Material Design specification.

A fully custom framework and complete set of reusable components were created for this project.
