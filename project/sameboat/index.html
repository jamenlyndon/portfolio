Same Boat

Full stack development of peer support forum for an Australian startup company.

Whilst definitions might differ, peer support describes a situation where people in the same boat get together to talk over issues they have in common.

Why is peer support so effective in helping people cope with problems? Only someone in the same boat can really understand what it's like.

This website features online user accounts, a custom made login system, social media style posts / likes / comments and much more.

This was a great project to be a part of. It's always nice to work on something for a good cause.

Made as a contractor for Margin Media.
