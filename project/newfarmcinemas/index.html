New Farm Cinemas

Design and development of an eCommerce website for a famous heritage listed cinema complex.

A very popular Brisbane landmark, New Farm Cinemas has been an epicentre of social activity and pop culture since 1921.

New Farm Cinemas screens a combination of mainstream movies and quality arthouse films.

Integrated with Veezi for ticket purchasing and session times.

This project was really fun to be a part of. It's not often you have the opportunity to work with such a cool client and make a website like this.

It was online for many years and sold cinema tickets extremely well. Unfortunately the company was eventually bought by "5 Star Cinemas" and this website was taken offline. Heart-breaking stuff, as I loved this project a lot.

Made as a contractor for Ruby6.
