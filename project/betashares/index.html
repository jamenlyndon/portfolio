Betashares

Design and development of a complex modern design system for an enterprise client.

Betashares is an Australian fund management company with $50 billion under management and over 1 million investors on their platform.

I was the technical lead on this project, responsible for delivering a design system which included a full suite of components, reusable website areas, a Figma design library, coding standards, deployment processes and much more.

This design system comes together to create the entire client facing website and provide a solid foundation for rapidly designing and developing new pages and functionality moving forward.

This was integrated into the existing product via CI/CD on multiple high traffic websites.

I was also responsible for maintaining and updating the existing product while the new design system was being developed and implemented.

I'm very happy to have had the opportunity to work with such an amazing client and product.
